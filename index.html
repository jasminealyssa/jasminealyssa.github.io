Jasmine Alyssa Creative

Boutique Editing Services for Wedding Photographers

No matter what state I live in, the subject I'm studying, oe the people I'm around, I've never been able to stop being creative. During my time at a liberal arts institution I was introduced to the world of Photography and how moments could be captured in a way that makes them eternal. However, as a full time student in Connecticut who happens to be from Kentucky, my ability to pursue the capturing of love could no longer be expressed through my lens, but I chose to stay connected in the photography business. I decided my time and abilities were better spent focusing on the parts of photography that I absolutely love: EDITING!
It wasn't long before I realized how crucial a personal relationship with your photo editor was to growing a photography business! Not only do I give photographer back HOURS of thei workflow, but I open up space in their schedules for them to focus on the parts of the business that only they can do! My job is to match a photographer's editing style so that I remain invisible and their photography business can continue to flourish.
